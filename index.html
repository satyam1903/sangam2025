    Tournament Rules & Regulations

    • Team Size: Each team must consist of exactly 11 players.
    • Overs:
      • Day 1 (8 overs per side): 2 overs per bowler.
      • Day 2 (Final 15 overs per side): 3 overs per bowler.
    • Attire: Sports shoes are mandatory for all participants. Teams are responsible for maintaining uniformity in their attire, but wearing the same color is not mandatory.
    • Umpires: All umpire decisions will be final and binding.
    • Scoring: All scoring will be done via Cricheroes, and the Man of the Series (MOS), Best Bowler, Batsman, and Fielder will be automatically decided through the app.
      • Note: If the Best Bowler or Best Batsman is awarded MOS, the second-best performer in that category will be given the award instead.
    • Format: The tournament will be played in a knockout format.
      • Day 1: Two matches.
      • Day 2: One final match.
